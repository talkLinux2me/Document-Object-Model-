About Us

Thank you for joining us today. We are a walking route guidance app with live tracking and up to date safety warnings. Volume and accesibility is always enabled and may only be disabled via voice recognition. Walk, ride, glide or roll at your own pace and enjoy a worry free experience as you explore your city.

Choose from these different themes:

  1. Nature Enthusiast
  2. Hike Master
  3. Peace Warrior
  4. Thrill Seeker

Comments, Feedback, or Suggestions?

Feel free to submit any accommodation request via email or phone. Is there another route theme you would like to enjoy? Let us know!
