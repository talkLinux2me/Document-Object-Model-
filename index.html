About Us

Thank you for joining us today. We are a walking route guidance app with live tracking and up to date safety warnings. Volume and accessibility is always enabled and may only be disabled via voice recognition. Walk, ride, glide or roll at your own pace and enjoy a worry free experience as you explore your city.
